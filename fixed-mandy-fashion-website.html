A Vision Rooted in Craftsmanship

Our Story

Located in the heart of Ragama, Sri Lanka, Mandy Fashion is a boutique dedicated to celebrating heritage through handcrafted batik clothing.

Each piece we create is thoughtfully designed to honor this tradition while adapting it to fit modern fashion sensibilities.

Whether it's a stunning saree, a flowing dress, or custom casual wear, we ensure that every garment is more than just clothing—it's a cultural statement.

Artful Tradition

Our designs preserve the intricate wax-resist dyeing method that transforms fabric into art, passed down through generations.

Peacock Symbolism

Our logo—a vividly feathered peacock beside "MF"—reflects beauty, pride, and the vibrant diversity of our batik patterns.

Community Connection

We're more than a fashion brand—we're a community-centered boutique offering personalized experiences that celebrate our shared heritage.

The Batik Process

Discover the ancient art of batik creation that makes each piece uniquely beautiful.

1

Design

Artists create unique patterns that blend tradition with modern aesthetics.

2

Wax Application

Hot wax is applied to fabric in intricate patterns using traditional tools.

3

Dyeing

Fabrics are immersed in vibrant dyes, with waxed areas resisting the color.

4

Finishing

Wax is removed, fabric is washed, and garments are expertly crafted with care.
